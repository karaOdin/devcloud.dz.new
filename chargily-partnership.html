About Our Partner

Who is Chargily?

Chargily is an Algerian fintech company providing borderless payment solutions for individuals and businesses. Their platform supports online payments, mobile top-ups, and digital financial services across the country.

Payment Gateway

Accept CIB and EDAHABIA cards online with secure checkout. Visa and Mastercard support coming soon.

Mobile Top-Up Distribution

Automated distribution of prepaid telecom credits for Mobilis, Djezzy, Ooredoo, and Idoom operators.

Secure Infrastructure

99.9% uptime SLA with bank-grade security, real-time webhooks, and full transaction reconciliation.

Open-Source APIs

Free, well-documented RESTful APIs with SDKs and pre-built plugins for popular platforms.

Chargily Pay

Accept Payments Online Instantly

Chargily Pay is an electronic payment gateway that enables your business to accept CIB and EDAHABIA card payments through your website, app, or simple payment links. No website required to start collecting payments.

  • Secure checkout integration for websites and apps
  • Payment links — share via SMS, email, or social media
  • No subscription fees, no activation costs
  • Free API access with open-source plugins
  • Real-time transaction dashboard and unlimited payouts

Chargily Pro

Automated Mobile Top-Up Distribution

  • Support for Mobilis, Djezzy, Ooredoo, and Idoom (ADSL + 4G)
  • Secure RESTful API with real-time webhook notifications
  • Centralized management dashboard with sales analytics
  • Transaction reconciliation and detailed reporting
  • High-volume capacity with 99.9% uptime guarantee
