CloudRoutes

The management platform for bus companies

CloudRoutes handles everything from QR ticketing and passenger wallets to agent operations, fleet GPS tracking, financial reconciliation, and daily reporting — replacing paper-based workflows with a single digital system.

Platform Capabilities

Everything a bus company needs, nothing it does not

Ten modules built around real daily operations at Algerian transport companies.

01

Every Ticket, One Scan Away

QR-based ticketing that works instantly — even with damaged or dirty codes. No paper waste, no manual counting, no disputes.

  • Instant QR code generation and validation
  • Works with scratched, faded, or partially damaged codes
  • Eliminates paper ticket printing and storage
  • Automatic fare calculation per route and zone

02

Passengers Pay Their Way

Smartphone users pay through a mobile app with a digital wallet. Passengers without smartphones use physical prepaid cards. Same balance, same system — nobody left behind.

  • Digital wallet via mobile application
  • Physical prepaid card for non-smartphone users
  • Unified balance across both payment methods
  • Top-up at kiosks, agents, or online

03

Know Where Every Bus Is

Live GPS tracking for your entire fleet. Monitor routes, speeds, and driver behavior in real time. Full journey history stored for review.

  • Real-time position updates on a live map
  • Speed monitoring and geofence alerts
  • Complete journey history and replay
  • Driver behavior scoring and reporting
04

Agents That Work Anywhere

Receivers collect fares on buses. Controllers sell cards at kiosks. Both work offline — transactions sync automatically when back online.

  • Offline-first architecture for unreliable connectivity
  • Automatic sync when connection is restored
  • Role-based access: receivers, controllers, supervisors
  • Per-agent transaction logs and shift tracking
