CloudRoutes: Bus Company Management Platform

CloudRoutes is a management platform built for urban and suburban bus companies. It handles everything from QR ticketing and passenger wallets to agent operations, fleet GPS tracking, financial reconciliation, and daily reporting.

  • Financial reconciliation and daily settlements
